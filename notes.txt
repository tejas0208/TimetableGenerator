Detecting clashes in timetable
-------------------------------
endtime-starttime overlaps endtime2-starttime2, roomid no clash 
endtime-starttime overlaps endtime2-starttime2, teacherid no clash 
endtime-starttime overlaps endtime2-starttime2, classid	no clash (if batchid absent)
endtime-starttime overlaps endtime2-starttime2, batchid no clash
edtime-starttime overlaps endtime2-starttime2, batchid no clash
